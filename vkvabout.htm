The Virtual Krewe of Vaporwave (VKV), New Orleans’ first and only virtual Mardi Gras krewe, is a collective of audiovisual artists, disruptors, memelords, and thinkfluencers from New Orleans and the internet.

Membership in VKV is pseudonymous, and individual members are encouraged to keep the details of their membership a secret. VKV members are required to set their personal and professional identities aside when creating art with VKV.
