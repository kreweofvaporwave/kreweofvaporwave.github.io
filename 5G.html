KREWE OF VAPORWAVE UNVEILS “#SMARTYOURCITY” CAMPAIGN WITH 5G TOWER ACTIVATION

 Krewe of Vaporwave Smart City’s Fundraiser Benefit Gala Experience Feb 2


“To Benefit Smart City’s Everywhere”
  • -–-–- Krewe of Vaporwave -–-–-


The Virtual Krewe of Vaporwave (us, we) placed pairs of bright and visible stickers on new 5G towers in the French Quarter.

The first sticker, a high-grade, weatherproof ISO radiation warning sticker placed at eye level, is identical to radiation stickers that were placed by installation professionals high up on the antennas, far out of public view. You’re welcome.

The second sticker–the one with the code that you scanned–is scientifically intended to be quickly washed away by the weather, and you know what they say about the weather in New Orleans:

“Whatever it is they say”

The Krewe of Vaporwave takes no official opinion on 5G.

The krewe promises more acts of public service that contrast the naive carpetbaggery of techno-futurism with the reality of our city’s crumbling public infrastructure, including the new stuff.

This year’s “Smart City’s” Fundraising Gala Benefit Event Spectacular will be held at Margaret Place on February 2, 2023.
