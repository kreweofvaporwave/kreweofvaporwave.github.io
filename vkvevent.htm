VKV hosted its first ever IRL Ball during Carnival 2017, drawing over 250 revelers to the Daiquiri Lounge in Gert Town. 

VKV also hosts fundraising events in and around New Orleans to raise awareness of the krewe and to help fund our Carnival projects. 

Jazz Cup: Runneth Over - Saturday, May 5, 2018

The highly anticipated annual fete of the iconic Solo Jazz Cup drew over 400 people to the Drifter Hotel in New Orleans. 

The World’s Unfair - Friday, February 2, 2018

The Krewe of Vaporwave and New Orleans Airlift combined forces to deliver a satirical re-creation of the 1984 World’s Fair, the last World’s Fair on the North American continent.
